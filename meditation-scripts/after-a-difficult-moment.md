# After a Difficult Moment

**Category:** Reset · **Duration:** 4 min · **Moment:** Right after something difficult

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/after-a-difficult-moment.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Something hard has just happened.

[pause 8s]

Before anything else — before deciding what to do, what to say, what to think — take this pause.

[pause 12s]

Place a hand on your chest, or your belly. Wherever feels steadying.

[pause 12s]

Notice that you are still here. Still breathing. Still you.

[pause 12s]

Take a slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 12s]

Again. In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 14s]

It is okay to feel what you are feeling. You do not need to fix it now.

[pause 14s]

The body has been holding tension. Let it soften where it can.

[pause 12s]

Around the eyes. The jaw. The shoulders. The hands.

[pause 16s]

You are not the moment that just happened. You are the one who lived through it.

[pause 14s]

Take one more breath. Soft. Long.

[pause 12s]

There is no right thing to do next. Only the next small thing.

[pause 12s]

Begin there. Gently.

[pause 6s]

# Between Meetings

**Category:** Reset · **Duration:** 2 min · **Moment:** In the gap between two meetings

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/between-meetings.mp3`

[pause 8s]

[pause 8s]

# Before Responding

**Category:** Reset · **Duration:** 3 min · **Moment:** Before sending the message or speaking

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-responding.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Pause before you answer.

[pause 6s]

Feel your feet. Or your seat. Whatever is supporting you.

[pause 10s]

Let the first breath soften the body.

[pause 8s]

Take it slowly. In...

...and out.

In...

[pause 12s]

In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 14s]

Trust that.

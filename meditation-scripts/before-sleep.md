# Before Sleep

**Category:** Rest · **Duration:** 8 min · **Moment:** In bed, before closing your eyes

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-sleep.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Lie down. Make yourself comfortable.

[pause 8s]

Let the body be heavy. Let the bed hold you.

[pause 10s]

You do not need to do anything for the next few minutes. You do not even need to sleep, yet.

[pause 12s]

Take a slow breath in through the nose...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 10s]

Again. In...

[pause 4s]

...and a longer breath out.

[pause 12s]

Begin to soften the body, piece by piece.

[pause 8s]

Soften the forehead. The space between the eyebrows.

[pause 12s]

Soften the eyes. The cheeks. The jaw.

[pause 12s]

Let the tongue rest. The mouth. The throat.

[pause 12s]

Let the shoulders melt down, away from the ears.

[pause 12s]

Soften the chest. The belly rising and falling on its own.

[pause 12s]

Let the arms be heavy. The hands. The fingers.

[pause 12s]

Let the hips settle. The legs. The knees. The feet.

[pause 14s]

Take a long breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a longer breath out.

[pause 12s]

If the mind is still busy, that's okay. You don't have to chase it. Let it talk while the body rests.

[pause 14s]

The day is finished. You have done enough.

[pause 12s]

You do not have to plan tomorrow. You do not have to remember everything. You do not have to be anyone.

[pause 16s]

Just here. Just breath. Just rest.

[pause 14s]

Each breath out, a little heavier.

[pause 12s]

Each breath out, a little softer.

[pause 16s]

Sleep is not something you make happen. It comes, when the body is ready.

[pause 14s]

Let the body be ready.

[pause 12s]

One last quiet breath.

[pause 10s]

Rest now.

[pause 6s]

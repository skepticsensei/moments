# After Training

**Category:** Practice · **Duration:** 5 min · **Moment:** After a workout or training session

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/after-training.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

The work is done. Or this part of it.

[pause 8s]

Slow down. Let the breath catch up to the body.

[pause 12s]

Stand or sit. Whichever feels right.

[pause 10s]

Soften everything that has been working hard.

[pause 12s]

The hands. The jaw. The shoulders. The forehead.

[pause 14s]

Take a slow breath in through the nose...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out through the mouth, if you'd like.

[pause 14s]

Let the heart settle. There is no rush.

[pause 12s]

Notice the body — not what it did, but how it feels now.

[pause 16s]

Heat. Breath. Pulse. Fatigue. Energy. All of it is the body talking.

[pause 14s]

Listen for a moment.

[pause 14s]

What did the body teach you today?

[pause 16s]

You do not need an answer right now. The body keeps its lessons.

[pause 14s]

Take one more long breath.

[pause 8s]

Drink some water when you're done. Move slowly back into the day.

[pause 10s]

Thank yourself for the work.

[pause 6s]

[pause 3s]

Bring your attention to the soles of your feet.

[pause 6s]

Notice where they meet the floor. The pressure, the warmth, the weight.

[pause 9s]

You do not need to imagine anything. Just feel what is there.

[pause 8s]

Heel. Arch. Ball of the foot. Toes.

[pause 10s]

Notice that you do not have to hold yourself up. The ground is doing it.

[pause 9s]

One slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

# Gratitude Before Rest

**Category:** Rest · **Duration:** 3 min · **Moment:** Before sleep

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/gratitude-before-rest.mp3`

---

[pause 3s]

Before sleep, one quiet practice.

[pause 6s]

You don't need to make a list. You don't need to perform.

[pause 8s]

Take a slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 8s]

Bring to mind one thing from today that held you. Not a big thing. A small one.

[pause 14s]

A drink of water. A quiet minute. A face you love. A moment of relief.

[pause 14s]

Just one.

[pause 8s]

Let it be present in the body. Notice if anything softens.

[pause 10s]

You don't have to thank anyone but the moment itself.

[pause 10s]

It happened. It was given. You received it.

[pause 10s]

One more long breath.

[pause 6s]

Sleep well.

[pause 4s]

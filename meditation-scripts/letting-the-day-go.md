# Letting the Day Go

**Category:** Rest · **Duration:** 6 min · **Moment:** In the evening

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/letting-the-day-go.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

The day is ending.

[pause 6s]

Whatever happened, it is no longer happening.

[pause 10s]

Sit, or lie down, somewhere comfortable.

[pause 10s]

Let the body settle. Heavy. Soft. Supported.

[pause 12s]

Take a slow breath in through the nose...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 10s]

Again. In...

[pause 4s]

...and a longer breath out.

[pause 12s]

Bring the day to mind, gently. Like reading the cover of a book without opening it.

[pause 14s]

You don't need to relive it. Just notice it.

[pause 12s]

Was there a moment that was good? A small one is enough.

[pause 14s]

Was there a moment that was hard? Let it be there too.

[pause 14s]

You don't have to fix any of it now. The day is past.

[pause 12s]

Picture yourself setting it gently down. Like putting a book on a shelf.

[pause 14s]

It is no longer in your hands.

[pause 12s]

The body softens. The shoulders, the jaw, the hands.

[pause 14s]

What today asked of you, it is no longer asking.

[pause 10s]

What tomorrow will ask, it has not asked yet.

[pause 10s]

Right now, there is just this breath.

[pause 10s]

Long. Slow. Quiet.

[pause 12s]

Let the day go.

[pause 10s]

One more breath.

[pause 5s]

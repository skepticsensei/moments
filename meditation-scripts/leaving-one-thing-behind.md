# Leaving One Thing Behind

**Category:** Transition · **Duration:** 4 min · **Moment:** Between one task and the next

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/leaving-one-thing-behind.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

There is something you have been carrying.

[pause 8s]

A thought. A worry. A list. A conversation that will not let go.

[pause 12s]

You can set it down for a moment. Not forever. Just now.

[pause 12s]

Picture it as something simple. A stone. A folder. A shape in the hand.

[pause 14s]

Notice its weight.

[pause 10s]

Now imagine placing it on a table beside you. Not throwing it. Not pushing it away. Just placing it down.

[pause 16s]

Take a slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 12s]

It is still there. You can pick it up later if you choose.

[pause 14s]

But right now, your hands are empty.

[pause 12s]

Notice what it feels like to be without it, even for these few breaths.

[pause 16s]

This is what creating space feels like.

[pause 12s]

When you return, you can decide what to pick up. What to keep carrying. What to leave.

[pause 14s]

One more breath.

[pause 6s]

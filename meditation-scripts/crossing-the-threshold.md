# Crossing the Threshold

**Category:** Transition · **Duration:** 3 min · **Moment:** At a doorway, real or symbolic

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.

[pause 4s]

You are at a threshold. Real or imagined.

[pause 8s]

A doorway. A change. A boundary between one thing and the next.

[pause 12s]

Pause here for a moment. Thresholds deserve to be noticed.

[pause 12s]

Feel your feet on the ground.

[pause 10s]

Take a slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 10s]

Behind you, what was. Ahead of you, what is becoming.

[pause 14s]

You do not have to be the same on both sides.

[pause 12s]

When you cross, cross gently. With breath. With intention.

[pause 14s]

One more breath.

[pause 4s]

Now step through.

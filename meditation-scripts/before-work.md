# Before Work

**Category:** Begin · **Duration:** 4 min · **Moment:** Before opening the laptop

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-work.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Before the work begins, take this small clearing.

[pause 6s]

Sit if you can. Both feet on the floor.

[pause 8s]

Soften the jaw. Lower the shoulders. Unclench the hands.

[pause 10s]

Take a slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a slower breath out.

[pause 10s]

Again. In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 12s]

The list will wait. Nothing has been missed. You are arriving.

[pause 12s]

Now bring one thing to mind. The single most important thing for today.

[pause 12s]

Not the easiest. Not the loudest. The one that matters most.

[pause 14s]

Hold it gently. Let everything else fall into the background.

[pause 15s]

When you open your work, begin with this. Even for a few minutes.

[pause 12s]

The smaller things will follow.

[pause 10s]

One more breath.

[pause 4s]

In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 6s]

Begin.

[pause 4s]

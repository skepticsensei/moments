# Yi Jin Jing-Inspired Reset

**Category:** Practice · **Duration:** 7 min · **Moment:** Mid-day, when the body feels stiff

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/yi-jin-jing-inspired-reset.mp3`

---

[pause 3s]

This is a short reset, inspired by the old practice of changing the tendons. Strengthening from the inside.

[pause 8s]

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Knees soft.

[pause 8s]

Lengthen the spine. Soften the shoulders. Let the arms hang.

[pause 8s]

Take a slow breath in through the nose...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 8s]

Now press the soles of your feet gently into the ground. Feel the connection.

[pause 10s]

Imagine the breath drawing up from the earth, through the feet, into the body. And on the out-breath, releasing back down.

[pause 12s]

Again. Up through the feet on the in-breath...

[pause 5s]

...and down through the feet on the out-breath.

[pause 12s]

Slowly raise the arms out to the sides. Palms down. As if holding the ground at hip height.

[pause 12s]

Breathe. Notice the small effort it takes to hold them there. Don't strain. Just notice.

[pause 14s]

After a few breaths, lower the arms slowly back to your sides.

[pause 12s]

Now turn the palms forward. Slowly raise the arms out in front of you, to chest height.

[pause 12s]

As if pushing gently into the air. Not hard. Just present.

[pause 12s]

Breathe. Three slow breaths here.

[pause 18s]

Lower the arms. Soften.

[pause 8s]

Stand for one quiet breath.

[pause 8s]

Now slowly raise both arms overhead. Palms up.

[pause 10s]

Imagine you are gently lifting the sky.

[pause 10s]

Three breaths here. Long and quiet.

[pause 18s]

Lower the arms slowly. All the way down.

[pause 10s]

Stand still. Notice what has changed.

[pause 12s]

The body remembers more than the mind does. Trust that.

[pause 10s]

One more long breath.

[pause 6s]

When you are ready, return to the day.

[pause 5s]

# When You Feel Overwhelmed

**Category:** Reset · **Duration:** 5 min · **Moment:** When the day has become too full

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/when-you-feel-overwhelmed.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Whatever is happening right now, you are allowed to slow down.

[pause 8s]

You do not have to carry all of it at once.

[pause 10s]

Place both feet on the floor. Or both hands flat on a surface. Something solid.

[pause 12s]

Feel that contact. Real. Steady. Beneath you.

[pause 12s]

One breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a longer breath out.

[pause 10s]

The body responds to a long out-breath. Even just one.

[pause 12s]

Again. In...

[pause 4s]

...and a longer breath out.

[pause 14s]

You are not behind. You are here.

[pause 12s]

Look around the room, slowly. Name three things you can see.

[pause 18s]

Now name one thing you can hear.

[pause 14s]

And one place in your body that is okay. A finger. A foot. The breath.

[pause 16s]

The list of things asking for you will still be there in a moment.

[pause 12s]

This minute belongs to the body. To the breath. To you.

[pause 14s]

One more breath. Slow. Long.

[pause 12s]

When you return, you will return softer.

[pause 10s]

Begin again, gently.

[pause 6s]

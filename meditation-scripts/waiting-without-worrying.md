# Waiting Without Worrying

**Category:** Return · **Duration:** 3 min · **Moment:** While waiting

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/waiting-without-worrying.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

You are waiting.

[pause 6s]

For something or someone. For a line to move. For a result. For time to pass.

[pause 12s]

The mind would like to fill this with worry. You can let it.

[pause 12s]

Or, you can use this small unclaimed moment.

[pause 10s]

Feel your feet. Feel your breath.

[pause 12s]

Notice anything you can see. The shapes around you. The colors. The light.

[pause 16s]

Notice anything you can hear. Near sounds. Far sounds. The space between them.

[pause 16s]

There is nothing you have to do here.

[pause 10s]

The thing you are waiting for will arrive when it arrives.

[pause 12s]

Until then, you are not behind. You are here.

[pause 10s]

One slow breath.

[pause 6s]

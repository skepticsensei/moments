# Before the Day Begins

**Category:** Begin · **Duration:** 5 min · **Moment:** Before stepping into the day

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-the-day-begins.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Welcome.

[pause 5s]

Before you reach for anything — before the first thought of the day takes you with it — return to yourself.

[pause 6s]

Feel the weight of your body. Where you meet the bed, or the floor, or the chair.

[pause 8s]

Take a slow breath in through the nose...

[pause 4s]

...and a long, soft breath out.

[pause 8s]

Again. In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 10s]

The day has not begun yet. You have. There is no rush.

[pause 10s]

Soften the muscles around your eyes. Soften the jaw. Let the shoulders drop.

[pause 12s]

Notice that the body has already been working without you having to ask. Heart. Lungs. Breath.

[pause 12s]

Without trying to change anything, listen to one full breath. The whole shape of it.

[pause 15s]

Quietly, gently, ask: how do I want to meet this day?

[pause 10s]

Not what you must do. How you want to meet it. Calm. Steady. Curious. Patient. Kind.

[pause 12s]

Choose one small intention. A single word. Let it settle into the body.

[pause 18s]

One more breath together.

[pause 4s]

In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 6s]

When you are ready, open your eyes.

[pause 4s]

The day is waiting. Step into it gently.

[pause 5s]

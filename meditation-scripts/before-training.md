# Before Training

**Category:** Begin · **Duration:** 3 min · **Moment:** Before the session begins

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-training.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Take a moment before practice begins.

[pause 6s]

Stand or sit. Feel the floor beneath you.

[pause 8s]

Drop the shoulders. Lengthen through the spine — without rigidity.

[pause 10s]

One slow breath in...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 10s]

Bring your attention into the body. Not what it should do. What it is, right now.

[pause 12s]

Notice tightness, looseness, energy, fatigue. All of it is information.

[pause 12s]

Set a small intention for this session. To learn something. To be present. To move with care.

[pause 14s]

The work begins not when you start moving, but when you arrive.

[pause 12s]

You are arriving now.

[pause 10s]

One more breath.

[pause 4s]

When you are ready, begin.

[pause 4s]

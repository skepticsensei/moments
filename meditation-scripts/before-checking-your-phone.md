# Before Checking Your Phone

**Category:** Begin · **Duration:** 2 min · **Moment:** Before reaching for the phone

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-checking-your-phone.mp3`

---

[pause 3s]

Before the noise begins, take this minute. It is yours.

[pause 7s]

One slow breath in through the nose...

[pause 4s]

...and a long breath out.

[pause 8s]

Notice your body. Your hand, perhaps already reaching. Let it rest.

[pause 9s]

Your attention is yours, first.

[pause 10s]

Another breath. Slower. Softer.

[pause 9s]

When you do pick up the phone, do it on purpose. Not because the day pulled you. Because you chose to.

[pause 10s]

One last breath.

[pause 5s]

You are ready.

[pause 4s]

# Before Stepping Onto the Mat

**Category:** Practice · **Duration:** 3 min · **Moment:** Before practice begins

> Recording notes: pad ~5s of silence at start and end. Speak slowly.
> `[pause Xs]` markers are silent — don't speak them.
> Save the recording as: `public/audio/before-stepping-onto-the-mat.mp3`

---

[pause 4s]

Stand tall. Without becoming rigid.

[pause 8s]

Let your breath settle into your center. Down through the belly. Down to the ground.

[pause 12s]

Feel both feet, evenly. Spread the toes if it helps.

[pause 12s]

Soften the shoulders. Let the arms hang.

[pause 12s]

You are about to begin training.

[pause 10s]

This means you are about to learn something. Even if today is the same form, the same drill, the same routine.

[pause 14s]

You are not the same person who trained yesterday.

[pause 12s]

Step onto the mat with respect. With attention. With a willingness to learn.

[pause 14s]

One more breath. In...

[pause 4s]

...and out.

[pause 6s]

Begin.

[pause 4s]
